Built On

React

Used to build the game interface, allowing dynamic and interactive gameplay elements that update in real time.

Node.js

Powers the backend, handling game logic, player connections, and server-side processes efficiently.

Express.js

Manages the server and API routes, making communication between the frontend and backend smooth and organized.

MongoDB

Stores player data, scores, and game progress in a flexible and scalable database.

Target Audience

The game is designed for players who enjoy fast-paced online action and adventure, with a strong focus on competition and teamwork.

  • Ages 15–30
  • Fans of PvP gameplay
  • Enjoy team-based strategies
  • Prefer quick matches
  • Like pixel art & medieval themes

Purpose

01. Team Strategy

Encourage teamwork and communication by making players coordinate attacks, defend allies, and adapt to shifting battle conditions.

02. Competitive Action

Create fast-paced matches where every decision matters, rewarding skill, timing, and tactical thinking in every encounter.

03. Replayability

Deliver varied experiences through different play styles and match outcomes, keeping players engaged and motivated to improve.

Escability

Our architecture is built to grow from casual matches to massive tournaments. New players connect fast, servers adapt in real time, and performance stays smooth even at peak battle hours.

Elastic Players

Matchmaking scales by demand, keeping queues short from 10 players to 10,000 online warriors.

Smart Load Balance

Live sessions are distributed across nodes to prevent lag spikes during intense fights.

Ready For Expansion

New regions and game modes can be deployed without downtime, maintaining nonstop gameplay.
